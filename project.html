GABI Fashion Brand Identity

Brand Identity

Crafting a contemporary fashion identity for the modern professional

GABI is a sustainable fashion brand targeting young professionals who value both style and environmental responsibility. The challenge was to create an identity that communicated modern sophistication while maintaining an approachable, human quality that would resonate with millennial consumers.

Client

GABI Fashion

Year

2023

Services

Brand Identity, Packaging

Deliverables

19+ Brand Assets

Primary logo mark in brand colors
Business stationery suite
Product packaging design
Digital application mockup

Brand Strategy

The GABI brand needed to stand out in the crowded sustainable fashion market while appealing to style-conscious professionals. Our strategy focused on three core pillars: Modern Sophistication, Sustainable Luxury, and Approachable Premium.

We conducted extensive market research to understand the target audience's values and preferences, which revealed a desire for brands that balanced eco-consciousness with high-end aesthetics. This insight drove our design direction, resulting in an identity that feels both elevated and accessible.

Visual Identity

The solution was a clean, geometric logo paired with a warm yet refined color palette that could flex across various applications from digital to physical packaging. The custom wordmark features subtle rounded corners to soften the otherwise precise geometry, creating a balance between premium quality and accessibility.

Brand Elements

  • Typography: A contemporary serif typeface for headlines paired with a clean sans-serif for body text creates hierarchy while maintaining readability across applications.
  • Color Palette: Warm neutrals with bold accent colors that work across digital and print applications while maintaining the brand's premium feel.
  • Patterns & Textures: Subtle organic textures inspired by sustainable materials add tactile depth to physical applications.
  • Photography Style: Clean, well-lit product shots with natural textures in the background reinforce the brand's sustainable values.

Implementation

The new identity was rolled out across all touchpoints including packaging, e-commerce, social media, and physical retail spaces. The cohesive system allows for flexibility while maintaining strong brand recognition.

Since launch, GABI has seen a 40% increase in brand recognition and a 25% increase in online sales, with customers specifically citing the distinctive branding as a key factor in their purchase decision.

Pure Bliss Spa Branding

Luxury Branding

Creating a serene visual identity for a premium wellness retreat

Pure Bliss Spa is a luxury wellness retreat specializing in holistic treatments and premium relaxation experiences. The branding needed to immediately communicate serenity, natural healing, and understated luxury to appeal to high-end clients seeking rejuvenation.

Client

Pure Bliss Spa

Year

2022

Services

Brand Identity, Packaging, Signage

Deliverables

7 Key Visual Assets

Main logo with watercolor effect
Spa interior signage
Spa product line packaging
Treatment menu design

Design Approach

We developed a soft, organic logo mark with a custom watercolor effect that evokes the fluidity of relaxation. The elegant typography and soothing color palette were inspired by natural elements - stone, water, and botanicals - creating a cohesive sensory experience across all touchpoints.

The identity system was designed to feel like a breath of fresh air, with ample white space and organic shapes that suggest movement and flow. Every element works together to create a sense of calm and tranquility.

Visual Elements

  • Logo: Custom watercolor logo with hand-painted textures that suggest fluidity and relaxation
  • Typography: Elegant serif typeface with subtle ligatures for a refined, luxurious feel
  • Color Palette: Mineral-inspired tones that evoke natural elements and create a soothing atmosphere
  • Patterns: Subtle organic patterns based on water movement and botanical elements
  • Materials: Premium paper stocks and finishes were selected for physical applications to enhance the luxury experience

Implementation

The branding was applied across all touchpoints to create a fully immersive experience:

  • Interior Signage: Subtle illuminated signs with the watercolor logo create a serene ambiance
  • Product Packaging: Minimalist packaging with soft-touch finishes and debossed details
  • Digital Presence: A calming website with smooth animations that mimic water movement
  • Collateral: Treatment menus and brochures with luxurious paper stocks and delicate typography

The result is a distinctive brand identity that has helped Pure Bliss Spa establish itself as a leader in the luxury wellness space, with a 35% increase in repeat clients since rebranding.

Coastal Breeze Brand Identity

Lifestyle Branding

Capturing the essence of coastal living through design

Coastal Breeze is a lifestyle brand for beach lovers, offering apparel, accessories, and home goods inspired by coastal living. The branding needed to capture the carefree spirit of the seaside while maintaining a premium aesthetic that would appeal to both vacationers and year-round coastal residents.

Client

Coastal Breeze

Year

2023

Services

Brand Identity, Packaging, Retail Design

Deliverables

3 Core Brand Concepts

Primary logo with wave motif
Apparel tag design
Retail store signage

Brand Concept

The solution was a custom wordmark with a subtle wave element in the letterforms, paired with a bright, airy color palette and beach-inspired patterns. The typography combines a relaxed sans-serif with nautical-inspired details, creating a look that's both contemporary and timeless.

We developed three distinct concepts before arriving at the final direction, each exploring different aspects of coastal life - from rugged nautical to breezy tropical. The selected concept strikes the perfect balance between premium and approachable.

Brand Components

  • Logo System: Custom typographic logo with wave-inspired details in the letterforms
  • Color Palette: Seaside-inspired tones (aquas, sands, deep blues) that work across products
  • Patterns: Nautical stripes, wave motifs, and shell-inspired textures
  • Typography: Relaxed sans-serif primary typeface with a nautical-inspired secondary font
  • Photography Style: Bright, airy lifestyle shots with authentic coastal settings

Applications

The branding was implemented across all customer touchpoints:

  • Apparel Tags: Natural recycled materials with subtle embossing
  • Product Packaging: Minimalist designs that let the products shine
  • Retail Spaces: Airy, beach-inspired store designs with natural materials
  • Digital Presence: Responsive website with fluid animations mimicking wave movement
  • Marketing Materials: Seasonal campaigns that adapt the core identity

Since launching the new identity, Coastal Breeze has seen a 50% increase in brand recognition and a 30% increase in sales, with customers praising the cohesive yet flexible branding system.
